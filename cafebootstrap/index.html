FEATURED PRODUCTS

  • California Raisin Bread
    Our famous raisin bread has been praised by concord residents and visitors alike! Our Tree Leaf standard guarantees the softest and moistest bread.
  • Hazelnut Craze Cupcake
    We must admit, this cupcake has hooked some of our customers and we couldn't be happier! Our Hazelnut Craze Cupcake is adored for the nutty chocolate piping, signature look, and the iconic Ferrero™ Rocher on top.
  • Foggy Frappe
    Feeling like a refreshing drink that satisfies your coffee craving? Our Foggy Frappe is perfect for you! Topped with caramel or chocolate drizzle and whipped cream, this frozen treat will have you coming back for more.

    Similar to our classic croissants, our chocolate croissants are just as fresh and even more delicious! If you're looking for something a little different than the usual, we suggest our delicious chocolate chip croissants.

    In the mood for an iced coffee? We got you. Any flavor, roast, or crazy combination available is at your disposal. When it comes to our iced coffee's we're the best in the business.

    Trek through this muddy and delicious chocolate layered mousse dessert for a limited time only! Our Layers of the Forest is our newest creation, but if we get enough love for it, it'll be a permanent resident of our jungle!
