Floyd Myers

Owner of 3 Girls 1 Chef

3 Girls 1 Chef is a family owned catering company owned by Floyd Myers and his family of five. He has three wonderful daughters that inspired the name for his catering start-up: Aria, Arissa, and Analise. The three girls support Floyd with his endeavors, so that he can continue to feed customers with his delicious cooking for many years to come. 3 girls 1 chef has been in operation fo 37years    and continues to thrive with its unique take on traditional Jamaican-style cuisine; enticing both new and old customers alike. Those who know Floyd understand that he is an impassioned chef who strives to provide high quality, delectable food for all his patrons, family, and friends to enjoy. Floyd and his family continue to thrive in the catering sphere and are projected to only see their success flourish thanks to customers like you! .
